One Farmer. A Shocking number of t-shirts

You can grow cotton in places where land and labor are cheap. You can grow it in places that are close to the countries — Colombia, Indonesia, Bangladesh — where the Planet Money T-shirt was made. Yet most of the cotton in the Planet Money T-shirts was grown in the U.S.

In fact, the U.S. exports more cotton than any country in the world. Can you think of three reasons why U.S. cotton is king?
